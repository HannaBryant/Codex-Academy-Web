What is CodeX Academy?

CodeX Academy is an intensive coding technology training program focused on preparing students for real-world careers across multiple areas of software development. Our programs emphasize hands-on learning, modern technologies, and industry best practices to help students build job-ready skills. At CodeX Academy, we foster a supportive, accountabilty-driven learning environment where problem-solving, collaboration, and continuous improvement are our core values. CodeX Academy prepares all students to adapt, grow and succeed in an ever-evolving tech landscape.
